Meet The Maker

My name is Drew Rigby, I’m 22 and am local to Utah. My parents instilled in me a thrifty lifestyle, and I grew up chasing the coolest antique stores on family road trips. One I fell in love with in 2021 is Confetti Antiques and Books! Highly recommend if you're in the area.

I’m a college student pursing a degree in Experience Design and Management. I picked up journaling as a more concrete habit as I lived out of state doing mission work, and now I love to express my creativity through collages as I journal. My love for reading and writing stories began at a young age, and I became fascinated with books. This passion led me to take a bookbinding course earlier this year. I now work in the Conservation Lab in my local library, where I use those bookbinding skills to repair and conserve books and other library materials.

The History of The Bindery

I’ve been collecting vintage books for years, but it wasn’t until that bookbinding course that my passions for vintage books and journaling crossed paths. My final project for the class was to deconstruct a vintage book and rebuild it as a journal, including some of the pages from the original book. And thus, Ex Libris Bindery was born. Now I turn vintage books into journals that you get to enjoy! These journals, along with others in various forms, are handmade by me. I work from my desks both at home and at work to fill the world with more art, more love, and more firsthand accounts of the human experience.
