Vintage Books Turned Into Journals

A journal with both blank pages and vintage pages (I try to keep them from the original book but sometimes the pages are non-harvestable) set into the case of the vintage book. These books have been used as gratitude journals, grimoires, and more! What will your story be?

A Touch of The Traditional Journals and Books

Books made in traditional binding styles, including hollow-back, flat-back, paper case, coptic, and more! These journals will likely only include blank pages but are highly customizable. Books made in these styles have been used as everyday journals, cookbooks, small business logs, and even D&D journals. Ask for examples of these bindings!

On The Go Mini Journals

These simple journals are easy to slip in a folder, backpack, or pocket! They’re great to have on hand, contain 8-12 pages, and come in large, medium, or small sizes. These journals have been used to keep notes in class, church, and in daily adventures. Where will they take you?

For the Aesthetic Vintage Paper Packs

Because so much of my work revolves around taking old books apart, I have tons of old paper on hand! This pack contains between 15 and 20 pieces of vintage paper, which could include old book pages, vintage greeting cards, old photos, vintage recipe cards, and beyond! These pages are perfect for collage creation and add the perfect touch to journals, penpal letters, photo setups, or home decor.
